About Us

At Sat Defenders, we're dedicated to transforming the landscape of defense intelligence through advanced satellite technology and AI-powered analysis. Born out of a commitment to global security, we combine state-of-the-art satellite monitoring with real-time AI processing to identify threats as they emerge.
